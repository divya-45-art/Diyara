Who We Are

The Diyara Story

Born from craft. Built for life.

Our Origins

Craft Passed
Down Through Time

Diyara began in 2024 with a single belief — that the finest things in life are made by hand, not machines. Our founder, a leather enthusiast with a passion for traditional Indian craftsmanship, set out to create goods that last not just years, but lifetimes.

We work with a small collective of master artisans across India, each carrying decades of experience in hand-stitching, edge-burnishing and vegetable tanning. Every Diyara piece is a collaboration between heritage and modern design.
